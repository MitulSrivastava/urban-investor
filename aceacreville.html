Project Overview

Ace Acreville is a premium 100-acre integrated township located on Yamuna Expressway, Sector 22A, Gautam Buddha Nagar. Developed by Ace Group, this project offers residential plots only with a distinctive neo-classical design theme featuring grand arches, fluted columns, stately entryways, and symmetrical landscapes. The township is designed with wide internal roads, elegant piazzas, and sculpted promenades, approved by YEIDA with all legal documentation and RERA registered.
