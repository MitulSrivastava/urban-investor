Project Overview

Gaur Butterfly is a premium collection of 3 & 4 BHK "Landmark Residences" located on Yamuna Expressway, Sector 22D. Developed by Gaur Group, this project features a distinctive Art Deco-inspired design theme with butterfly motifs throughout, expansive 8.5-acre landscape greens, and a 75,000 sq.ft. clubhouse - the largest in the region. The project spans 4.85 hectares (~12 acres) with 7 iconic towers in Phase 1 launch.
